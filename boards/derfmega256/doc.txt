# Overview
deRFmega128 and deRFmega256 are a family of modules produced by Dresden Elektronik.
deRFmega128 modules are based on [ATmega128rfa1](http://ww1.microchip.com/downloads/en/DeviceDoc/Atmel-8266-MCU_Wireless-ATmega128RFA1_Datasheet.pdf),
deRFmega256 modules are based on [ATmega256rfr2](http://ww1.microchip.com/downloads/en/DeviceDoc/Atmel-8393-MCU_Wireless-ATmega256RFR2-ATmega128RFR2-ATmega64RFR2_Datasheet.pdf) 
MCUs. Both types include 16MHz main and 32K RTC crystalls and (depending on module type) integrated or not integrated 2.4GHz antenna.

These modules are available in three variants: [deRFmega128-22](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega128-22m00.html)/[deRFmega256-23](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega256-23m00.html) M00 with integrated antenna,
[deRFmega128-22](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega128-22m10.html)/[deRFmega256-23](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega256-23m10.html) M10, and [deRFmega128-22](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega128-22m12.html)/[deRFmega256-23](https://www.dresden-elektronik.de/produkt/24-ghz-avr-derfmega256-23m12.html) M12 without integrated antenna 
as separate products or as a main part of other products like [RaspBee](https://phoscon.de/en/raspbee) and [ConBee I](https://phoscon.de/en/conbee)
